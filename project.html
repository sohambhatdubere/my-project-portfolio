Automation of Electro-Etch Marking Process

@ GE Aerospace

Developed a compact 3-DOF robotic arm to automate stencil-based electro-etch marking and replace inconsistent manual steps. Implemented closed-loop motion for precise dwell and contact pressure, planned trajectories in Robo-Analyser, and integrated a simple electronics stack. The prototype stabilized the process and helped drive a large reduction in non-conformance with high end-effector accuracy.
