Automation of Electro-Etch Marking Process

@ GE Aerospace

Designed a compact 3-DOF robotic arm to automate stencil-based electro-etch marking. Planned trajectories (Robo-Analyser), modeled the arm (CATIA), and integrated Bluetooth control with a simple electronics stack. The prototype stabilized dwell/pressure and is projected to cut non-conformance from ~10% to ~1% at 7,200 parts/month.

Development of 6-Jaw Chuck for Anti-Polishing Ring

@ Cooper Corporation

Co-developed a six-jaw workholding concept for thin-walled anti-polishing rings with internal slots. Consolidated >6 operations into a single clamp, distributing forces via inner/outer jaws and C-axis jaws to maintain ~20 ¬µm circularity. Result: fewer re-clamps, better yield, and ~50% lower total cost.
