Automation of Electro-Etch Marking Process

@ GE Aerospace

Developed a compact 3-DOF robotic arm to automate stencil-based electro-etch marking, replacing manual operations prone to inconsistency. Implemented PI-controlled motion for precise dwell and pressure, achieving 80% reduction in non-conformance and 90% end-effector accuracy. Coordinated Lean Kaizen initiatives to integrate the prototype into production, cutting in-process time by 24%.

Development of 6-Jaw Chuck for Anti-Polishing Ring

@ Cooper Corporation

Co-designed a six-jaw chuck mechanism for machining thin-walled anti-polishing rings with internal slots. Engineered inner/outer jaw synchronization and uniform clamping force distribution to maintain ~20 ¬µm circularity and eliminate multi-setup errors. Consolidated more than six machining operations into a single setup, reducing tooling cost by ~50% and improving repeatability for high-precision rings.
