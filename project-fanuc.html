Fanuc i100C Welding Cell

Fronius power source · Mitsubishi FX5U PLC · Mitsubishi HMI · Ethernet/IP + CC-Link — same application as STM32 cell.

What

  • Built a standard robotic welding cell using a Fanuc i100C with a Fronius power source.
  • Cell control via Mitsubishi FX5U PLC and Mitsubishi HMI for operator workflows.
  • Industrial comms over Ethernet/IP (robot/welder) and CC-Link (peripherals), mirroring the STM32 cell’s application flow.

How

  • Programmed Fanuc jobs for path execution, torch clean, and synchronized weld start/stop with PLC interlocks.
  • FX5U logic for sequence control, safety checks, Fronius handshake, and peripheral coordination over CC-Link.
  • Mitsubishi HMI screens for job selection, cycle control, alarms/history, and maintenance routines.
  • Validated IO timing and ready-state checks prior to commissioning for predictable startup.

Results

  • Delivered a robust, modular welding cell equivalent in functionality to the STM32-based system.
  • Clear separation of responsibilities: Fanuc (motion/weld), FX5U (cell orchestration/safety), Mitsubishi HMI (visibility/control).
  • Standardized job structures and Ethernet/IP + CC-Link backbone simplify commissioning and future scaling.
