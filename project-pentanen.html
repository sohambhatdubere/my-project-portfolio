Graduate Production Automation Engineer

@ Pentanen Engineering & Copper Cloud IoT

Why

  • Manual set-ups and limited diagnostics on the bending and sorting lines created idle time and scrap.
  • Needed PLC/SCADA with simulated validation to accelerate commissioning and reduce rework.
  • Targets: higher throughput on the Crippa bending cell and better sorting quality with faster fault recovery.

How

  • Programmed Siemens PLC/SCADA for a 6-axis Delta robot on a Crippa bending line; validated motion/IO with Factory I/O at prototype stage.
  • Built TIA Portal HMI for weight-based sorting; used WinCC Trend Configuration to analyze cycle weight/quality and tune thresholds.
  • Implemented structured alarms, diagnostics, and guided recovery with role-based navigation and training prompts.
  • Led induction/commissioning, fixturing, and operator training; captured feedback for iterative HMI/logic refinements.

Results

  • ~20% production efficiency gain on the Crippa bending line through Delta-robot PLC/SCADA controls validated in simulation.
  • ~15% improvement in sorting accuracy via TIA weight-based logic tuned with WinCC Trends.
  • ~30% downtime reduction from advanced alarm diagnostics and guided recovery on the HMI.
  • Faster ramp-up and adoption via commissioning support, fixturing, and technical training.
