Automated Crippa Bending Cell (6-Axis Delta Robot)

@ Pentanen Engineering & Copper Cloud IoT

What

  • Automated a Crippa bending line with a 6-axis Delta robot for material handling and cell coordination.
  • Programmed Siemens PLC/SCADA and validated sequences in Factory I/O during prototyping.
  • Delivered an HMI/SCADA layer with clear states, alarms, and diagnostics to support commissioning and training.

How

  • Implemented PLC sequences for infeed/outfeed, robot handshakes, cell interlocks, and safe mode transitions.
  • Mapped IO and coordinated motion timing for the Delta robot relative to the Crippa machine cycle.
  • Built SCADA/HMI screens for setpoints, status, alarms, and guided recovery; used Factory I/O to test logic and IO before site bring-up.
  • Led on-site induction, commissioning, fixturing, and technical training to stabilize production startup.

Results

  • ~20% production efficiency gain on the Crippa bending line through PLC/SCADA control validated in simulation.
  • Reduced idle time with synchronized robot–machine cycles and clearer alarms/diagnostics.
  • Faster ramp-up and smoother changeovers via structured HMI workflows and operator training.
