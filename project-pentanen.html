Graduate Production Automation Engineer

@ Pentanen Engineering & Copper Cloud IoT

Why

  • Manual set-ups and basic diagnostics on the bending and sorting lines caused idle time and scrap.
  • Needed a PLC/SCADA-driven solution with simulated validation to accelerate commissioning and reduce rework.
  • Target outcomes: higher throughput on the Crippa bending cell and better sorting quality with faster recovery from faults.

How

  • Programmed Siemens PLC logic and SCADA sequences for a 6-axis Delta robot on a Crippa bending line; validated motion and IO with Factory I/O during prototyping.
  • Built TIA Portal HMI screens for **weight-based sorting**, using **WinCC Trend Configuration** to analyze cycle weight/quality and tune thresholds.
  • Implemented structured alarms, diagnostics, and guided recovery, plus role-based navigation and training content.
  • Led on-site induction/commissioning, fixturing, and operator training; captured feedback for iterative HMI/logic refinements.

Results

  • ~20% production efficiency gain on the Crippa bending line through Delta-robot PLC/SCADA controls validated in simulation.
  • ~15% improvement in sorting accuracy via TIA weight-based logic tuned with WinCC Trends.
  • ~30% reduction in downtime using advanced alarm diagnostics and guided recovery on the HMI.
  • Improved ramp-up and customer adoption through commissioning support, fixturing, and technical training.
