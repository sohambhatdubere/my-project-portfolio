PR0001 – Multi-Stage Robotic Inspection & Sorting Cell

Educational training cell for automated visual inspection, material classification, and PLC–robot coordination.

What

  • Designed a training cell to perform multi-parameter inspection and sorting of mixed material parts.
  • Integrated photoelectric sensors, color detection, and material classification (metal / non-metal) using capacitive and inductive sensors.
  • Built to teach industrial sequencing, vision inspection, and robot–PLC handshaking concepts.

How

  • Configured a PLC-controlled conveyor that triggers robotic inspection cycles using sensor feedback.
  • The robot captures images for surface scratch / patch detection and classifies parts via color and material sensors.
  • Implemented step-based control logic for movement, inspection, and part placement in respective zones.

Results

  • Delivered a hands-on training cell illustrating integrated vision and sensor-based automation.
  • Simplified PLC–robot coordination enables students to study object detection, quality sorting, and process automation.
  • Provides a modular platform for expanding into IIoT and AI-vision applications in future coursework.
